## AI Capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Generate Playbooks" icon="plus">
    Tell Demiurg AI what you want to automate, and it will create a complete playbook with all necessary steps and error handling.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Compare Versions" icon="code-compare">
    See your current playbook and AI-generated changes side by side in the editor, making it easy to review and accept updates.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Modify Content" icon="pen">
    Ask AI to update specific parts of your playbook using natural language instructions.
  </Accordion>

## Agent Configuration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Basic Setup">
    Configure essential settings:
    - Email address
    - Phone number
    - Environment variables
    - Base instructions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Knowledge Setup">
    Define agent knowledge:
    - Domain expertise
    - Response guidelines
    - Business rules
    - Custom data
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Integration Setup">
    Connect necessary tools:
    - AI models (ChatGPT, Claude)
    - Communication channels
    - External services
    - Custom integrations
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Assign playbooks and activate the agent
  </Step>
</Steps>
