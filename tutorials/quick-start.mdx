<Tip>
  In less than 15 minutes, you'll have a working Demiurg agent that can handle automated workflows. This guide walks you through building your first human-like agent, creating a playbook, and designing automation pages.
</Tip>

## Step 1: Create an Agent

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/create-agent.png" alt="Agent Creation Interface" className="rounded-lg" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Agent Dashboard">
    From the main dashboard, click the **Create Agent** button in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Basic Settings">
    <div className="space-y-4">
      <div>
        <h4 className="font-medium text-primary dark:text-primary-light mb-2">Identity</h4>
        <ul className="list-disc list-inside space-y-1 ml-2">
          <li>Name and surname (e.g., "Sophia Chen")</li>
          <li>Professional title (e.g., "Customer Experience Specialist")</li>
          <li>Profile photo to represent your agent</li>
          <li>Personality and communication style</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      
      <div>
        <h4 className="font-medium text-primary dark:text-primary-light mb-2">Communication</h4>
        <ul className="list-disc list-inside space-y-1 ml-2">
          <li>Business email address</li>
          <li>Phone number for SMS or WhatsApp</li>
          <li>Social messaging channels</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Up AI Tools">
    <div className="space-y-4">
      <div>
        <h4 className="font-medium text-primary dark:text-primary-light mb-2">AI Models</h4>
        <p className="text-sm">Select from OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini models to power your agent's responses</p>
      </div>
      
      <div>
        <h4 className="font-medium text-primary dark:text-primary-light mb-2">Knowledge Base</h4>
        <p className="text-sm">Upload documents and resources to give your agent domain-specific expertise</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Create Pages

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/page-editor.png" alt="Page Editor" className="rounded-lg" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Pages to Playbook">
    In your playbook, click **"Add Page"** to create a new workflow or edit an existing template page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Build with Blocks">
    <div className="space-y-4">
      <div>
        <h4 className="font-medium text-primary dark:text-primary-light mb-2">Listen Blocks</h4>
        <ul className="list-disc list-inside space-y-1 ml-2">
          <li>WhatsApp messages</li>
          <li>Email received</li>
          <li>Scheduled events</li>
          <li>Form submissions</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      
      <div>
        <h4 className="font-medium text-primary dark:text-primary-light mb-2">Generate Blocks</h4>
        <ul className="list-disc list-inside space-y-1 ml-2">
          <li>OpenAI models</li>
          <li>Anthropic Claude</li>
          <li>Google Gemini</li>
          <li>Custom LLM integrations</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use Demiurg AI">
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/ai-assistant.png" alt="AI Assistant" className="rounded-lg" />
    </Frame>
    
    <div className="mt-4">
      <h4 className="font-medium mb-2 text-primary dark:text-primary-light">Natural Language Page Creation</h4>
      <p className="text-sm italic mb-2">"Create a page that greets customers, categorizes their inquiry by urgency and department, then routes it appropriately."</p>
      <p className="text-sm">Type what you want to build in plain language and Demiurg AI will generate a complete workflow</p>
    </div>
  </Step>
</Steps>

  <div className="font-medium mb-2">Key Points to Remember:</div>
  <ul className="list-disc list-inside space-y-1">
    <li>**Agents** are human-like virtual professionals with their own identity</li>
    <li>**Playbooks** are collections of related pages assigned to an agent</li>
    <li>**Pages** contain workflow logic built with Listen and Generate blocks</li>
    <li>**Listen blocks** trigger on incoming messages or events</li>
    <li>**Generate blocks** use AI models to process information and create responses</li>
  </ul>
</Check>
