<Check>
  <div className="font-medium mb-2">Key Points to Remember:</div>
  <ul className="list-disc list-inside space-y-1">
    <li>**Agents** are human-like virtual professionals with their own identity and expertise</li>
    <li>**Playbooks** are collections of related pages assigned to an agent</li>
    <li>**Pages** contain the actual workflow logic built with Listen and Generate blocks</li>
    <li>**Listen blocks** trigger on incoming messages, emails, or events</li>
    <li>**Generate blocks** use AI models to process information and create responses</li>
  </ul>
</Check>
