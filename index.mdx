## Craft Your First Agent in Minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe Your Agent">
    Be specific about what your agent should know, its personality, and what tasks it should perform.
    
    ```
    "I need an AI agent that can analyze financial markets and provide trading insights based on current trends and historical data."
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review & Refine">
    Demiurg will instantly build your agent. Test it with a few questions and refine if needed.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/agent-creation.png" alt="Agent Creation Process" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Share & Deploy">
    Deploy your agent to web, mobile, or share it with your team or the community.
    
    <Check>Access your agent anywhere</Check>
    <Check>Collaborate with team members</Check>
    <Check>Integrate with your existing tools</Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Demiurg Works

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/demiurg-workflow.png" alt="Demiurg Workflow" />
</Frame>

<Info>
  Demiurg combines state-of-the-art language models with a proprietary agent framework that translates natural language descriptions into fully-functional AI systems.
</Info>

1. **Natural Language Understanding** - Your description is analyzed to identify key capabilities, personality traits, and knowledge domains
2. **Agent Architecture Design** - Demiurg builds a custom architecture tailored to your needs
3. **Automatic Integration** - Tools, knowledge bases, and behaviors are configured automatically
4. **Continuous Improvement** - Agents learn and improve with each interaction
