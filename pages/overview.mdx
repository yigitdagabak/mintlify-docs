<Tip>
  Pages are individual workflows that define specific automated processes for your agents. They combine specialized blocks with natural language instructions to create powerful, AI-driven automation.
</Tip>

## What are Pages?

Pages are the fundamental building blocks where you design specific workflows for your agents. Each page defines a complete process that starts with a trigger (Listen block) and includes the logic for how to handle and respond to that event using AI (Generate block).

## Creating Pages

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to a Playbook">
    Open an existing playbook or create a new one in your agent's dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add a New Page">
    Click "Add Page" button within your playbook to create a new workflow.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Creation Method">
    Decide whether to build manually or use Demiurg AI to generate the page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="For AI Generation">
    If using AI, provide a detailed description of what you want the page to do and click "Generate."
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="For Manual Creation">
    Give your page a name and start adding blocks using the slash command.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/listen-block-menu.png" alt="Listen Block Selection Menu" className="rounded-lg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Blocks">
    Set up each block's settings based on your workflow requirements.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/listen-block-config.png" alt="Listen Block Configuration" className="rounded-lg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Instructions">
    Write clear instructions for how the AI should analyze and respond to triggers.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save and Test">
    Save your page and use the testing tools to verify its functionality.
  </Step>
</Steps>
