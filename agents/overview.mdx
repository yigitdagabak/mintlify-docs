## What are Agents?

Agents are AI-powered assistants that act as virtual team members capable of handling specific tasks and workflows. Each agent has its own identity, communication channels, knowledge base, and capabilities defined by the playbooks assigned to them.

## Creating Your First Agent

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Agents Dashboard">
    From your main dashboard, select "Agents" in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Create Agent'">
    Use the button in the top right corner to start the agent creation process.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Basic Details">
    Set your agent's name, purpose, and basic identity information.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Up Communication Channels">
    Configure email and messaging options for your agent.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Assign Playbooks">
    Select the playbooks that define your agent's capabilities.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review and Launch">
    Verify your agent configuration and activate it to start handling tasks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Clear Identity">
    Define a consistent, purpose-driven identity that aligns with your brand and resonates with users.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Comprehensive Knowledge">
    Provide thorough information and training materials to enable accurate and helpful responses.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Focused Scope">
    Design agents with well-defined responsibilities rather than attempting to handle too many diverse tasks.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Escalation Paths">
    Create clear pathways for complex issues to be routed to human team members when necessary.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Ongoing Refinement">
    Regularly review agent performance and user feedback to make continuous improvements.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Communication Style">
    Craft a tone and communication approach that suits your audience and business context.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
