## What are Agents?

Agents are AI-powered virtual professionals that act as dedicated team members capable of handling specific tasks and workflows. Each agent has a name, role, communication style, and specialized skills defined by the playbooks assigned to them.

## Creating Your First Agent

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Agents Dashboard">
    From your main dashboard, select "Agents" in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Create Agent'">
    Use the button in the top right corner to start the agent creation process.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Define the Person">
    Set your agent's name, title, profile picture, and professional background.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Up Communication">
    Configure email address, phone number, and messaging channels.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Assign Expertise">
    Select playbooks that define your agent's skills and capabilities.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Activate Your Team Member">
    Review the configuration and launch your new virtual professional.
  </Step>
</Steps>
