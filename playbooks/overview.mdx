## What are Playbooks?

Playbooks are collections of related pages that work together to handle specific functions or domains of knowledge. Think of playbooks as specialized skill sets you can assign to your agents to give them particular capabilities.

## Creating Your First Playbook

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Playbooks">
    From your main dashboard, select "Playbooks" in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Create Playbook'">
    Use the button in the top right corner to start the playbook creation process.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Define Basic Information">
    Add a name, description, and category for your playbook.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set priority levels, notification preferences, and other global settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Pages">
    Create new pages or add existing ones to build your workflow collection.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Publish and Assign">
    Publish your playbook and assign it to relevant agents.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<div className="space-y-4 mb-6">
  <div>
    <h4 className="font-medium text-primary dark:text-primary-light mb-1">Start with User Journeys</h4>
    <p className="text-sm">Map out entire processes from the user's perspective before building individual pages.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div>
    <h4 className="font-medium text-primary dark:text-primary-light mb-1">Focus on Related Functions</h4>
    <p className="text-sm">Keep playbooks focused on a specific domain rather than mixing unrelated capabilities.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div>
    <h4 className="font-medium text-primary dark:text-primary-light mb-1">Build for Reusability</h4>
    <p className="text-sm">Design playbooks that can be assigned to multiple agents with minimal customization needed.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div>
    <h4 className="font-medium text-primary dark:text-primary-light mb-1">Include Error Handling</h4>
