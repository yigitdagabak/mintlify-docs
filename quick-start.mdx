## Choose Your Approach

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Using AI">
    ### Create with Demiurg AI
    
    1. Navigate to Demiurg AI tab
    2. Click "Create New Playbook"
    3. Describe your automation needs
    4. Review and deploy generated playbook

    <Card title="Example">
      "I need a workflow that handles customer support tickets:
      - Categorize incoming emails
      - Route urgent issues to the right team
      - Send acknowledgments
      - Follow up after 4 hours if no response"
    </Card>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Manual Setup">
    ### Build Your Own
    
    1. Go to Playbooks section
    2. Choose "Create New"
    3. Design your workflow
    4. Test and deploy
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Basic Setup Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your First Agent">
    Configure your AI assistant:
    - Give it a name
    - Set up communication channels
    - Define knowledge base
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Integrations">
    Connect your tools:
    - Email
    - WhatsApp
    - Custom tools
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Test Your Setup">
    Verify everything works:
    - Send test messages
    - Check responses
    - Monitor workflow
  </Step>
</Steps>
