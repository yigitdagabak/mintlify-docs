## Main Interfaces

The Demiurg platform consists of two primary interfaces that you'll work with:

<Tabs>
 <Tab title="Agent Dashboard">
   <Frame>
     <img src="/images/agent-dashboard.png" alt="Agent Dashboard" className="rounded-lg" />
   </Frame>
   
   <div className="mt-4">
     <p>The <strong>Agent Dashboard</strong> is your command center where you:</p>
     <ul className="list-disc list-inside space-y-1 mt-2">
       <li>View and manage all your agents</li>
       <li>Monitor active conversations and resolved items</li>
       <li>See performance metrics in real-time</li>
       <li>Manage playbooks assigned to each agent</li>
       <li>Create new agents and playbooks</li>
     </ul>
   </div>
 </Tab>
 
 <Tab title="Page Editor">
   <Frame>
     <img src="/images/page-editor.png" alt="Page Editor" className="rounded-lg" />
   </Frame>
   
   <div className="mt-4">
     <p>The <strong>Page Editor</strong> is where you create and modify pages:</p>
     <ul className="list-disc list-inside space-y-1 mt-2">
       <li>Built on TipTap and Next.js</li>
       <li>Write in natural language</li>
       <li>Add specialized blocks with the slash command</li>
       <li>Get AI assistance in the right panel</li>
       <li>Preview and test your pages</li>
     </ul>
   </div>
 </Tab>
</Tabs>

With these two interfaces, you can create, manage, and monitor your entire automation workflow - from designing pages with the editor to deploying them through agents on the dashboard.
